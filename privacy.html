We only retain collected information for as long as necessary to provide you with your requested service. What data we store, we’ll protect within commercially acceptable means to prevent loss and theft, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use, or modification.

You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data. You can delete your account and all associated data directly within the app's settings. We don’t share any personally identifying information publicly or with third-parties, except when required to by law.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about how we handle user data and personal information, feel free to contact us at sshresthadeveloper@outlook.com.
